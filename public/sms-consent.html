Grooming Appointment SMS Consent Flow

How Customers Opt-In to SMS Notifications

This page documents the SMS consent workflow used on our grooming booking website at grooming.carterspetmarket.com.

Step 1 — Customer books online

Customer selects pet, service, date & time

Customers create an account, add their pet(s), choose a grooming service (Bath or Full Groom), and select an available appointment slot. After selecting a time, a confirmation modal appears.

Step 2 — SMS Opt-In (Confirmation Screen)

Explicit SMS consent checkbox — unchecked by default

Before confirming, the customer sees a clearly labeled SMS consent checkbox. The checkbox is unchecked by default — the customer must actively check it to opt in. Booking can be completed without opting in.

Actual consent UI shown during booking confirmation:

I agree to receive SMS text messages from Carter's Pet Market

By checking this box, you consent to receive automated appointment confirmation and ready-for-pickup text messages from Carter's Pet Market at the phone number on your account. Message frequency varies (typically 2 messages per booking). Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. Your phone number will never be sold or shared with third parties. Privacy Policy · Terms & Conditions

☝️

Checkbox is UNCHECKED by default. Customer must actively check it to opt in. SMS is completely optional — booking works without it.

Step 3 — Messages sent only with consent

Customer receives texts only if they opted in

If the customer checked the SMS box, they receive up to 2 automated text messages per booking: one appointment confirmation and one ready-for-pickup notification. No marketing messages are ever sent.

Sample Messages

Appointment Confirmation
Carter's Pet Market: Your grooming appointment for Max is confirmed for Feb 5 at 9:00 AM with Brittany. Reply STOP to opt out.
Ready for Pickup
Carter's Pet Market: Max is all done and looking great! You can pick up at your convenience. Reply STOP to opt out.

Compliance Summary

  • SMS opt-in checkbox is unchecked by default — requires affirmative action
  • Consent language clearly identifies the business name (Carter's Pet Market)
  • Message types are specified (appointment confirmation, pickup notification)
  • Message frequency disclosed (typically 2 per booking)
  • "Msg & data rates may apply" disclosure included
  • STOP to opt out and HELP for help instructions included
  • Links to Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions provided
  • Phone numbers are never sold or shared with third parties
  • SMS is optional — customers can book without opting in
  • No marketing or promotional messages are sent
  • Consent is collected per booking, not globally
